    About NCC

    Nepali Community Center formerly known as the Nepali Association of Western Washington, We are a non-profit, non-partisan group that aims to bring together civic, professional, and business leaders for advocating, fostering, and protecting the interests of our members.
    NCC works to connect and empower the Asian Nepali community through programs, services, and advocacy for people of all ages and all life stages.
    As a result, NCC can involve its full community in ways that incorporate and respect cultural traditions and values.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to create a vibrant and welcoming Nepali community center building that serves as a hub for cultural preservation, education, and empowerment.
    We are dedicated to fostering a sense of unity, belonging, and inclusivity among Nepali community members and the broader society.
    Through this center, we aim to celebrate our heritage, provide resources for personal and professional growth, and inspire a shared vision of a stronger, more connected community.
