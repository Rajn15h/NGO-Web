    About Founder

    The Nepali-American Community Center (NACC) in Washington, DC, was founded by members of the DC Metro Nepali community along with various organizations and supporters worldwide. The NACC was established in 2019 as a nonprofit organization with the aim of preserving and promoting Nepali identity, culture, and values within the United States. Key figures in the leadership include Dr. Madan Uprety, Chairman of the Executive Committee, and Arun Rupakheti, Chairman of the Board of Trustees. The center is dedicated to facilitating social, educational, cultural, spiritual, and sports activities to promote multiculturalism and social harmony in Northern Virginia​ (Nepali Center)​​ (NECC USA)​. The NACC is a significant hub for the Nepali community, organizing various cultural events such as the Newari festival "Ya Mahri Punhi" and the annual Dashain Tihar festival. These events help maintain and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Nepal within the American context
